  • If you think you have what it takes, dazzle your way through with your talent and charisma to claim the coveted titles under the big top of the carnival circus!
    No. of Participants: 02 (1 male & 1 female)
    Venue: Round 1 & 2 - AV Hall
    Round 3 & 4 - Don Bosco Oratory stage

    Rules

    ROUND 1: TALENT ROUND
    Participants have to showcase their talent in a performative manner.
    Each participant gets a maximum of 2 minutes to showcase their talent on stage.
    Any props, instruments and any other requirement for the performance must be arranged by the participant itself.
    ROUND 2: SURPRISE ROUND
    Will be revealed on the day of the event to the participants.
    ROUND 3: RAMP WALK & INTRODUCTION
    10 participants will be shortlisted (5 boys & 5 girls).
    Participants must walk down the ramp dressed elegantly, creatively, and presentably.
    Following the walk, participants must introduce themselves and their outfit.
    Judging criteria: Ramp Walk, Stage Presence and Creativity of the Outfit.
    ROUND 4: QUESTION ROUND
    In the final stage, every contestant will have a question asked by the judges that will evaluate their persona, attitude and promptness.
